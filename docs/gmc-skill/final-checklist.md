# Final Checklist

Use this before saying the store is ready for GMC review.

## Public Site

- No broken key internal links: contact, policies, products, footer, tracking if present.
- Contact page has working email link and phone link if shown.
- Business identity is clear.
- Footer links are logically grouped.
- No duplicate headings or obvious layout bugs.
- No unsupported reviews, star ratings, badges, warranties, guarantees, urgency, fake scarcity, or promotions.
- Cart text does not promise information that is not actually visible.
- Announcement bars and trust strips are factual.
- Product pages show coherent title, price, currency, variant, description, and policy references.
- FAQ matches policies exactly where it repeats facts.

## Policies

- Shipping policy includes country, cost, processing time, delivery time, cutoff if claimed, duties/taxes if relevant, tracking only if true.
- Return/refund policy includes return window, eligibility, damaged goods, return shipping cost, refund processing time.
- Terms/legal pages match business identity and market.
- Privacy/contact details match site and Shopify settings.

## Shopify Settings

- Store name/business details match site and GMC.
- Contact email/phone match public site.
- Address matches legal pages and GMC.
- Currency and market match target country.
- Shipping settings match policy and GMC.
- Returns settings or policy match GMC.
- Domain is primary and matches GMC.
- Product SEO fields are clean if used by feed app.

## Feed App

- Connected to correct Merchant Center.
- Domain matches store.
- Currency matches store/market.
- Product count expected.
- Product warnings/ineligible count checked.
- Title/description mapping understood.
- Metadata/SEO field sync performed after SEO changes.
- Full feed resync performed after important changes.
- Queue complete before final GMC check.

## Merchant Center

- Business info correct.
- Website verified and claimed.
- Countries only include intended markets.
- Shipping complete, visible, correct country, cost, delivery time, product scope.
- Returns valid, correct country, return window, return fee.
- Product source correct country/language/feed label.
- Product list shows correct currency and source.
- Product detail sample shows current title, description, URL, price, currency, availability, country, source, and recent update.
- Product shipping calculator shows correct destination, shipping service, cost, and currency.
- Product attention tab shows no unresolved product-specific issue beyond the global reviewable issue.

## Go

Say the user can request review when all of these are true:

- Site, Shopify, policies, feed app, and GMC values match.
- GMC product detail has propagated corrected feed data.
- Remaining issue is a reviewable global policy status.
- No unresolved warnings were found that you can actually fix.

Use wording like:

`You can request review now. I cannot guarantee Google's decision, but I found no remaining site/Shopify/feed/GMC inconsistency in the checked surfaces.`

## No-Go

Do not greenlight review if:

- GMC still shows old product data.
- Shipping/returns/currency differs between site, feed, and GMC.
- Product feed and product page differ on key fields.
- Business info differs between site, Shopify, and GMC.
- The site contains unsupported reviews, guarantees, badges, discounts, urgency, or trust claims.
- A link to a key policy/contact page is broken.
- A policy lacks a required field that the site or GMC relies on.

Say exactly what is blocking review and what must be fixed or allowed to propagate.
